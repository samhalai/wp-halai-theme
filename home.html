Hands-on skills in all stages of product development including research, ideation, validation, design, development, management, analytics, visualization and evaluation. Domains include health & wellness, social media, analytics, telecommunication and embedded systems.

I am currently the Director of User Experience at Limeade where I help define the future of healthcare incentive systems. I have worked in corporate research at FUSE Labs at Microsoft Research and the Visual Communication Lab at IBM Research and academic research at University of Michigan. I have also worked on core products with millions of users like MSN Messenger and Amdocs CRM.

I am excited by the opportunities at the intersection of society and technology and am the first person in the world with a Masters in Social Computing. I am originally from Bombay where I also did my undergrad in Computer Engineering. I enjoy paragliding, geocaching, kayaking, hiking and travel.
